Festival Policy
What You Can Bring
  • Blankets/towels
  • Two factory sealed water bottles (max. 1L)
  • Empty drinking containers
  • Empty hydration packs (max. 2.5L)
  • Clear backpacks (clear plastic or vinyl)
  • Fanny packs
  • Binoculars
What You Can Not Bring
  • Weapons of any kind (including any explosives such as fireworks)
  • Illegal substaces
  • Alcohol
  • Glass or cans of any kind
  • Professional video or audio recording equipment (including drones)
  • Motor vehicles of any kind (motorcycles, bicycles, skateboards, etc.)
  • Laser pointers
  • Umbrellas, selfie sticks, totems, flags
  • Spray paint, paint markers, Aresols
  • Large containers (picnic baskets, coolers, etc.)
  • Lawn furniture or inflatables
  • Tents
  • Pets
  • Threatening apparel and signs
  • Illegal vending and unauthorized/unlicensed vendors are prohibited
